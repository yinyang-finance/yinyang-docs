Farms

Farms are used to mint the initial supply of $YIN and $YANG. Once the supply has been fuly minted, the farms will be definitively closed. Farms have been designed to offer opportunities of varying risks.

Yin Farms

To earn the initial supply of $YIN, you can deposit the following tokens:

  • $YANG-WCANTO Velocimeter LP, 40x multiplier
  • $YANG, 10x multiplier
  • $cBonk, 5x multiplier
  • $ETH, 3x multiplier
  • $NOTE, 1x multiplier

Yang Farms

To earn the initial supply of $YANG, you can deposit the following tokens:

  • $YIN-WCANTO Velocimeter LP, 40x multiplier
  • $YIN, 10x multiplier
  • $cInu, 5x multiplier
  • $ATOM, 3x multiplier
  • $WCanto, 1x multiplier
